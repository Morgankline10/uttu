About Us

Uttu creates authentic and artful websites and branding materials for small to medium sized businesses that not only boost brand awareness and grow sales but create connective bonds between brands and consumers that enable long-run growth. We will establish trust with consumers through purely authentic and intuitive design that conveys the vision and passion of firms without any of the conventional, obnoxious feel of salesmen type pitches. We will provide continued maintenance and updates in order to allow business owners to focus on their big picture. We will actively seek to deploy emerging technologies in order to maintain brand relevance.

Morgan Kline, 22, is the founder of Uttu Designs, a Denver based web development firm with a focus on authenticity that builds trust. An innately curious adventurer, Morgan is always seeking to acquire new expertise and to apply that knowledge to a continually evolving product. Morgan and Uttu Designs will actively seek to understand your brand and create a web-based reflection of it that resonates with your clients.
